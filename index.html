about us

We are a group of gamers who love to play video games and have been playing them since we were very young. We believe multiplayer games are a great way to make new friends from all over the world. These games are also played on a competitive level, and tournaments are organized surrounding these games. E-sports is a term coined around these competitive games. Our target is to inform enthusiastic gamers, as well as sponsors, to enter this booming industry and find opportunities to do something that benefits them both mentally and financially.
